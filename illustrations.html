Illustrations

My current series is a mesh of my technical drawing skills and my painting skills. They are illustrations of influential people intricately drawn with a black ball-point pen. The clothing is then hand-painted with acrylic paint in what I can only describe as a blockprint-like pattern. (The pattern is flat, but I draw shadows over it to create depth.)
